Blood Pudding

Description

It's bloody tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 Thistle
  • 2 Bloodbags
  • 4 Barley Flour

Steps

  1. Grind the Thistle down into very small bits.
  2. Mix the blood from the Bloodbags with the Barley Flour and the ground Thistle.
  3. Cook the mixture in a Level 4 Cauldron.
  4. Enjoy!
