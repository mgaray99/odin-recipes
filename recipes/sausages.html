Sausages

Description

Links of savory, smoked meat.

Ingredients

  • 4 Entrails
  • 1 Boar Meat
  • 1 Thistle

Steps

  1. Grind the Boar Meat down into very small bits.
  2. Grind the Thistle down into very small bits.
  3. Mix the ground Thistle with the ground Boar Meat.
  4. Stuff the Entrails with the ground Boar Meat and Thistle mixture.
  5. Cook in a Level 2 Cauldron.
  6. Enjoy!
