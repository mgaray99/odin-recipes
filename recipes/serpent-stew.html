Serpent Stew

Description

Smells of honey and serpent...

Ingredients

  • 1 Mushroom
  • 1 Cooked Serpent Meat
  • 2 Honey

Steps

  1. Chop the Mushroom into small pieces.
  2. Chop the Cooked Serpent Meat into small pieces.
  3. Chop the Honey into small pieces.
  4. Cook everything in a Level 2 Cauldron.
  5. Enjoy!
